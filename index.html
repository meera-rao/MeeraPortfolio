Hello!

I am a UX designer, a problem solver trying to make sense of a chaotic world around me.

I have built a strong foundation of analytical and management skills over 10 years of experience in financial services where I saw how how businesses worked and what drives them. A desire for creativity and real consumer impact led me to start my own online store selling sustainable kids clothing. This experience of creating engaging and meaningful products in a structured way led me to UX design.

Finding the best possible solution for problems is where I find my joy.
